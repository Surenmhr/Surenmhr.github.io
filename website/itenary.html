Annapurna Circuit Trek: 15-Day Itinerary

The Annapurna Circuit Trek is a stunning journey through Nepal's majestic Himalayan landscapes, rich cultural villages, and challenging mountain passes. Below is the detailed day-by-day itinerary:

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu

Welcome to Nepal! Upon arrival, youâ€™ll be greeted at the airport and transferred to your hotel in Kathmandu. Rest or explore the bustling streets of Thamel.

Day 2: Drive to Besi Sahar (6-7 hours)

Embark on a scenic drive to Besi Sahar, the starting point of the trek. Enjoy views of lush valleys and rivers along the way.

Day 3: Trek to Bahundanda (1,310m, 5-6 hours)

Begin your trek through terraced fields and small villages, reaching the charming hilltop village of Bahundanda.

Day 4: Trek to Chamje (1,410m, 5-6 hours)

Climb through rugged terrain and beautiful waterfalls, entering the Manang region and arriving at Dharapani.

Day 5: Trek to Dharapani (1,960m, 6-7 hours)

Climb through rugged terrain and beautiful waterfalls, entering the Manang region and arriving at Dharapani.

Day 6: Trek to Chame (2,710m, 5-6 hours)

Pass through pine forests and quaint villages, with breathtaking views of Annapurna II and Lamjung Himal.

Day 7: Trek to Pisang (3,300m, 5-6 hours)

The trail offers stunning views of the mountains as you ascend to the picturesque village of Pisang.

Day 8: Trek to Manang (3,540m, 5-6 hours)

Cross alpine meadows and visit local monasteries. Spend the night in Manang, a vibrant cultural hub.

Day 9: Acclimatization Day in Manang

Rest and explore Manang to adjust to the altitude. Optional hikes to Gangapurna Lake or the Ice Lake.

Day 10: Trek to Yak Kharka (4,018m, 4-5 hours)

Gradual ascent through juniper forests and alpine pastures. Look out for yaks and blue sheep along the way.

Day 11: Trek to Thorong Phedi (4,450m, 4-5 hours)

Prepare for the high pass crossing as you trek to Thorong Phedi, the base camp for Thorong La Pass.

Day 12: Cross Thorong La Pass (5,416m) and Trek to Muktinath (3,800m, 7-8 hours)

The most challenging day of the trek! Cross the iconic Thorong La Pass, then descend to the holy temple of Muktinath.

Day 13: Trek to Jomsom (2,720m, 5-6 hours)

Descend through the Kali Gandaki Valley to Jomsom, known for its unique landscapes and strong winds.

Day 14: Fly to Pokhara

Take a short, scenic flight to Pokhara. Enjoy lakeside relaxation and optional sightseeing in the city.

Day 15: Drive/Fly to Kathmandu

Return to Kathmandu, where you can enjoy your final day exploring the city or shopping for souvenirs.
