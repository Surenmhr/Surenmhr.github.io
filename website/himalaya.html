Explore the Himalayas

Unveil the beauty and adventure of Nepal's majestic mountain range, offering thrilling experiences for all types of travelers.

The Majesty of the Himalayas

The Himalayas are not only the highest mountains in the world but also a rich cultural and natural treasure. From Everest to Annapurna, these mountains offer some of the most scenic views and the most thrilling adventure experiences on Earth.

Activities in the Himalayas

Trekking

Himalayan trekking is an unforgettable experience. Popular treks include the Everest Base Camp trek, the Annapurna Circuit, and the Langtang Valley trek. Hike through lush forests, cross high mountain passes, and witness breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks.

Mountaineering

For the experienced mountaineer, the Himalayas offer challenging expeditions to some of the world's highest peaks, including Mount Everest and Annapurna. Scale the world's tallest mountain or opt for lesser-known summits.

Adventure Sports

The Himalayas are not just for trekkers and climbers. For the adrenaline junkie, there are numerous adventure sports such as paragliding in Pokhara, white-water rafting on the Trishuli River, and mountain biking on rugged terrain.
