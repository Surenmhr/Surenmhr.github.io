Nature in Nepal

Nepal’s natural beauty is nothing short of spectacular. Home to eight of the world’s fourteen highest peaks, including the iconic Mount Everest, the country’s landscapes range from snow-capped mountains to subtropical jungles. Trekking routes like the Annapurna Circuit and Everest Base Camp attract adventure seekers from around the globe, offering stunning vistas and challenges for all levels. The lush forests of Chitwan and Bardia National Parks are teeming with wildlife, including rare species like the Bengal tiger, one-horned rhinoceros, and red panda. Whether you’re exploring serene mountain lakes, cascading waterfalls, or the roaring rivers perfect for rafting, Nepal’s natural wonders never cease to inspire awe.
